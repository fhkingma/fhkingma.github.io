About Me

I graduated from the Erasmus University Rotterdam obtaining a Master of Science (M.Sc.) degree in Econometrics and Management Science. The master's track I have undertaken was Operations Research & Quantitative Logistics. For my master's thesis, I was advised by Michel van de Velden. For the related research, I visited Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research lab (BAIR), where I was advised by Pieter Abbeel. I have a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) degree in Industrial and Applied Mathematics from Delft University of Technology. For my bachelor's thesis, I was advised by both Marco Loog and Frank van der Meulen.
